Cognitive Enhancement & Human Integration Trial (CEHIT)

By accessing or participating in the Cognitive Enhancement & Human Integration Trial (“CEHIT”), you agree to the following terms and conditions (“Agreement”), which govern your involvement in this research program operated jointly by the Neuropath Research Institute and Collins Library.



Eligibility

Participants must:

  • Be 18 years or older (or possess an alternative age equivalency authorization)
  • Have no active illness, injury, physical or mental condition which requires Overnight hospitalization or need of an support (such as service animals or physical disablility support)
  • Have read and understood the Participant Information Sheet and Informed Consent Form
  • Have no recorded history of irreversible thought entanglement, reality feedback loops, or archive contamination.

Voluntary Participation

Participation in the CEHIT study is entirely voluntary. You may:

  • Withdraw from the study at any time without penalty
  • Decline to answer any questions or complete any activity that causes discomfort

Your decision to participate or not will not affect your access to services at Collins Library or elsewhere.

Nature of the Study

The CEHIT study involves exposure to cognitive training exercises, textual analysis, and sensory perception activities. Study procedures may include:

  • To be tested with the new genetic enginering items
  • Engaging with guided reading materials
  • Participating in brief, non-invasive monitoring sessions
  • Completing questionnaires or psychological assessments

Confidentiality

All information collected will be treated as strictly confidential. Data will be:

  • Anonymized wherever possible
  • Stored securely on encrypted servers
  • Used solely for research purposes related to this study

Your name or identifying details will not be published in any reports or shared with third parties without your explicit permission, except as required by law.

Use of Data

By participating, you agree that:

  • Your anonymized data may be used in academic publications or presentations
  • Data may be retained for up to 10 years following the conclusion of the study
  • Your data will not be sold or used for marketing purposes

You may request access to, or the deletion of, your data at any time, subject to legal and regulatory limitations.

Compensation

There is no financial compensation for participation unless otherwise specified in writing. You may be reimbursed for reasonable travel expenses if pre-approved.

Withdrawal and Termination

The research team may also end your participation if:

  • You do not meet eligibility requirements
  • Continued participation is deemed unsafe for you
  • The study is discontinued or altered

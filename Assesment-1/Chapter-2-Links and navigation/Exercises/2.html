Key Information

DELTARUNE is a turn-based, RPG (role-playing) video game. featuring random battle encounters, puzzle solving, and a unique, thrilling, comedic storyline.


Plot overview

The player puppeteers a teenage human named Kris, who pairs up with a rude monster named, Susie.
Thier teacher sends the two to get chalk from the supply closet. However, the closet pulls them into a strange realm—the Dark World—where they meet the dark prince Ralsei.
The three of them are said to be the destined heroes who will seal the Dark fountains in the realm to perserve the balance of light and dark.


Characters

  • Kris - A quiet talented character, who the player controls as in the game.
  • Susie - A rude monster who like to talk with thier fist. Yet, She is able to assist a friend if they are in danger or troubled.
  • Ralsei - A humble kind prince who is willing to share on what they know of the dark world.
  • Lancer - A childish darkner who just wants to cool and awesome.
  • Noelle - A Friendly yet shy Lightner who is a hidden power card in the game.
